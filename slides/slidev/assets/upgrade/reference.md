## Reference Slide Contents

### Why do we need UpGrade?

- Accelerate research in a complex space
  - Different students have different needs
  - Standards can differ across states

- Group random assignment
  - Conduct RCTs in real classrooms
  - Supports adaptive software & asynchronous 
learning

- Address gaps in education research
  - Discover what works for subgroups
  - Reach diverse student populations, more quickly


### What is UpGrade and how does it work?

- Free and open source platform
- Can integrate with virtually any EdTech application
- Design and deploy educational experiments in minutes
- Seamless learning experiences for students
- Monitor learning outcomes and participant enrollment 
via web dashboard


### Experimental design for research needs

- Simple weighted randomization
- Factorial designs
- Within-Subjects
- Stratified random sampling
- Adaptive experiments

### How UpGrade Supports Education Research

- **Reach**: unprecedented, especially in K-12
  - Experiments embedded in curriculum–eliminates teacher burden
  - Quick turnaround to results means we can improve outcomes faster

- **Scale**: data is fast and easy to acquire
  - Used in over 25 studies across 5 platforms since 2022
  - 300,000 learners in Playpower Labs’ Battleship Numberline
  - 45,000 learners in a study via classroom-based MATHia instruction

- **Access**: harder-to-reach student populations
  - XPRIZE studies
    - Lame Deer School District (MT): 100% Native American
    - Las Cruces School District (NM): 79% Hispanic/Latinx
  - Emerging readers, ELLs
